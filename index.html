Vision

A Worldwide Network Of Sensors To Testify Good Water Quality

Water is a fundamental right for every living organism. Making water quality visible everywhere, at any time, is our mission.

Problem

The poor management of nutrients in aquaculture and land agriculture especially nitrate and phosphorous.

This leads to

  • An exhaustion of drinking water reserves worldwide

  • Natural Ecosystems degradation

  • Acute Shrinkage of Investment returns for all Natural-capital-based businesses

Solution

In-situ water quality sensor network to measure nutrient run-offs in real-time

Our solution uses a new sensing technology and machine learning to measure nutrients in fisheries and in agricultural drainage systems.

Our unique selling point is an affordable, accurate and autonomous sensor network for fish and plant nutrient management.
