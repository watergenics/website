Problem

The poor management of nutrients in aquaculture and land agriculture especially nitrate and phosphorous

This leads to

  • An exhaustion of drinking water reserves worldwide
  • Natural Ecosystems degradation
  • Acute Shrinkage of Investment returns for all Natural-capital-based businesses

Solution

In-situ water quality sensor network to measure nutrient run-offs in real-time

Our solution uses a new sensing technology and machine learning to measure nutrients in fisheries and in agricultural drainage systems. Our unique selling point is an affordable, accurate and autonomous sensor network for fish and plant nutrient management

Mission

Our mission is to foster the responsible use of water for food production
